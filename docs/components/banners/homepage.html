# Overview

The homepage banner is bespoke and may include a carousel of images and looping video or just a looping video.

# Variations

Carousel and video.

# Behaviours

Carousel: Every 3 seconds, the banner automatically slides from right to left and the related indicator dot activates. Selecting the controls will move forward and backward through the banner. Video: The video automatically plays on a loop. A button overtop allows the video to be manually paused and played.
